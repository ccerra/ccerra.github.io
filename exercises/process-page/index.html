Mystic Bites Brand Identity

Creating a memorable restaurant experience through branding and design.

Problem or Prompt

The goal was to create a restaurant brand that combines premium dining with mystery and interaction.

Solution or Hypothesis

I designed a complete visual identity focused on storytelling, curiosity, and memorable experiences.

Research and Analysis

I researched restaurant branding, competitors, and my target audience before beginning the design process.

Ideation

I explored different logo concepts, typography, and layout ideas before selecting the final direction.

Iteration

I refined the logo and visual identity through multiple revisions until the design felt balanced and consistent.

Design Principles

The final design follows hierarchy, contrast, balance, alignment, repetition, and consistency.

Mystery Forest

The trapdoor leads to a hidden mermaid kingdom beneath the Mystery Forest. Surrounded by glowing waters, ancient treasures, and magnificent underwater palaces, you discover a world lost to legend. The mermaids reward your curiosity with valuable treasures and a magical pearl before sending you home.

As you explore the cavern, you are amazed to find an entire secret world of mermaids living beneath the Mystery Forest. Beautiful underwater palaces shine in the distance, while friendly mermaids swim through the crystal-clear water and welcome you to their hidden kingdom.

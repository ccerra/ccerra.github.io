Mystery Forest

You sail across the glowing river and discover the legendary crystal resting on a golden pedestal. You become famous for solving the mystery of the forest.

Your boat carries you across the enchanted river until you reach a forgotten sanctuary hidden among the trees. There, a legendary crystal shines atop a golden pedestal, illuminating the forest with magical energy. The moment you claim the crystal, the spirits of the forest celebrate your courage and wisdom. The mystery has finally been solved, and your name will be remembered for generations.
